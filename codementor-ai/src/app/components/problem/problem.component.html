The Problem with Competitive Programming Training

Most competitive programmers train inefficiently and struggle to improve their ratings

🎲

Random Practice

Solving random problems without a structured plan leads to slow progress and wasted time.

🗺️

No Clear Roadmap

Without guidance, it's hard to know what topics to focus on or which problems to solve next.

📉

Hard to Track Progress

Difficult to measure improvement or identify patterns in your mistakes and weak areas.

😩

Easy to Burn Out

Unfocused training and lack of visible progress lead to frustration and eventual burnout.

🔄

Repeated Mistakes

Making the same errors across different problems without understanding the root cause.

❓

Unknown Weak Topics

Unaware of which algorithms and data structures need more practice and attention.
